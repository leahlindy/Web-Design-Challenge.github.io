Weather Patterns on Mars

A Martian day, 'Sol', is equivalent to 1d 0h 37m earth days.
The Insight project has open source data for the most recent 7 sols.

Insight data includes:

  • Average Temperature
  • Horizontal Wind Spead
  • Atmospheric Pressure
